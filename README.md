<h2>Biography & Career</h2>
<p><b>Kim*C</b> (often stylized with an asterisk) is a Ugandan Afro-pop and dancehall artist,
songwriter, and producer. He describes himself as a versatile artist, songwriter, music producer, and
studio director. He cites the late Ugandan music legend <b>Mowzey Radio</b> as a primary inspiration
for his musical style. Previously, he was signed to the record label <b>Bexx Nation</b>.</p>
